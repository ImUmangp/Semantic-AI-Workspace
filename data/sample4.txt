Content:

Overfitting occurs when a model learns noise in the training data, reducing its generalization to new data. Underfitting happens when the model is too simple to capture patterns in the data. Solutions: use regularization techniques, cross-validation, and ensure sufficient training data. Example: a deep neural network predicting stock prices may overfit if training data is limited.
